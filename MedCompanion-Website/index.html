Frequently asked questions

How accurate is the photo recognition?

Our AI achieves 95%+ accuracy in identifying pills by analyzing shape, color, imprint, and size. However, always verify with your pharmacist or prescription label for absolute certainty.

Is this a replacement for my doctor?

No. MedCompanion is educational support only. We help you understand and remember your medications, but we never provide medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions.

How does the AI chat work?

Our AI is guard-railed to only answer safe questions about medication information, side effects, and instructions. It refuses dangerous queries like dosage changes and redirects you to your doctor.

Is my health data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're HIPAA-compliant and never share your information with third parties. Your privacy is our priority.

Can I use this for multiple people?

With Premium Plus, you can enable Caregiver Mode to manage medications for family members. Caregivers get alerts and summaries while maintaining privacy.

Does it work offline?

Basic features like reminders and viewing your medication list work offline. Photo recognition and AI chat require an internet connection for real-time analysis.
